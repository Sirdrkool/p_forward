About Us

Purpose Forward is a Nigerian not-for-profit corporation that was established in early 2021 to accelerate the growth of grassroots, social purpose organizations. The founders have spent most of the last decade in senior roles in the not-for-profit sector with large community charities and hospotals.

While these established organizations plays a crucial role in making our communities better, they are not designed to innovate. The problems of our time require modern solutions to work in a system that has historically been designed with lots of processes to mitigate risk. Purpose Forward was founded to help grassroots organizations navigate this complex system and effectively scale to mazimize impact.
